Our Policies

OUR NAIL POLICIES

  • We only fix nails done at our salon.
  • We will fix nails done at our salon within a week of the original appointment, at no charge to the client. However, we only fix nails at the end of the day between 6:45 - 7:00 pm. A fee may be charged for nail fixes requested at any other times.
  • We do NOT fill BioGel or Liquid & Powder nails that were done at another salon.
  • We do NOT remove BioGel nails that were done at another salon.
  • We do not do refunds on any service.

OUR LASH POLICIES

All lash extension full sets require a 50% deposit at least 48 hrs before the scheduled appointment. Deposits are non-refundable if the appointment is cancelled/rescheduled with less than 48 hrs notice.

  • If there is less than 1/3 of the original lashes left, then it will be considered a full set, and not a fill.
  • We only do lash fills if the original lash set was done at our salon.
  • We do NOT do lash fills if the original lash set was done at another salon.
